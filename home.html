How can we help?

There are many small ways and changes with big impact that we, as consumers, can do to help protect our planet from plastic pollution.

  • Be a conscientious consumer - force yourself to gradually stop using disposable plastic and develop a habit of using reusable products, like bringing your own bags when going to the store, bringing your own cutleries and using a travel mug or bottle.
  • Use plastic alternatives – Fortunately nowadays we already have developed an array of replacement for plastic, such as bamboo, hemp and seaweed to name a few. There are plenty of materials that can do what plastic does without the negative impact to the environment.
  • Ditch the cling wrap - Unlike cling film that cannot be recycled, foil is recyclable. So, if you are using foil, make sure you put it in the recycling bin after use! Another alternative is the Beeswax Wrap.
